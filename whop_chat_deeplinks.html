How to fire a deeplink

Most reliable (works for every URL shape) — push the URL as the deeplink extra:

adb shell am start -n com.whop.whopapp.develop/com.whop.whopapp.MainActivity \
  --es deeplink "<URL>"

Tapping the links below on the device works too, but Android App Links only auto-verifies the /joined/* path, so non-/joined links may open the browser instead of the app — use the adb form for those.
