The REST calls behind the raffle flow, in order. Green ships from the native /api/v1 layer, blue from the GraphQL→REST proxy, and step 05 (the raffle deadline & draw) is the one resource still to build.

01

Start a raffle business

proxy
01_business.httpPOST /companies
POST /companies{ "title": "Jack's Raffles", "email": "jack@example.com" }→ { "id": "biz_8s2Kd…" }

One call, not two. The public accounts and companies endpoints are the same underlying Bot model — the native /api/v1/accounts controller literally exposes bots as "accounts," and both route through Companies::CreateCompany. Use companies.create as the canonical call. (End-user sign-in is separate — that's OAuth, not a business object.)
