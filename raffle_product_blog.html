The full flow, from "Bet on yourself" to a shareable raffle — five taps on your phone.

Raffles have always been one of the internet's best ways to turn a single great item into a crowd. Today we're making them a first-class way to sell on Whop — no spreadsheets, no DMs, no "trust me" screenshots. Open the app, bet on yourself, and your raffle is live in minutes.

How it works

Bet on yourself

Create your account from the home screen and you're in — no store setup, no approval queue to start.

Pick "Raffle"

On "What are you building?", choose Raffle alongside merch shops, courses, and communities.

Describe what you're raffling

Type "I want to raffle off…" and our AI drafts the whole product page — title, hype copy, and layout.

Review your product page

Add a photo, tweak the copy, set the ticket price. The page ships with a "Join raffle $5" button built in.

Share

Send the raffle card anywhere — group chats, stories, posts. Buyers tap in and grab a ticket.

Physical or digital, one-of-one or a bundle — if you can put a price on a ticket, you can raffle it. Like Timmy's legendary PS4.

A draw people can actually trust

The moment that makes or breaks a raffle is the draw. Whop picks the winner using verifiable randomness — every drawing comes with a published proof anyone can check. No "trust me," no off-camera spin. Buyers see the odds before they join and the receipt after you draw.

Get paid the moment it ends

Ticket revenue lands in your Whop balance as sales happen. Connect your bank once, and payouts run on the same rails creators already use across the platform. When you draw a winner for a physical prize, you collect their address and ship with tracking right from the app.

One important note: raffles and sweepstakes are legally regulated, and the rules vary by region. During beta we're rolling out carefully and reviewing eligibility — check that raffles are available and permitted where you and your audience are before you launch.
