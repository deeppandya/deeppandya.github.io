Raffles have always been one of the internet's best ways to turn a single great item into a crowd. The problem was the plumbing: collecting money in DMs, tracking entries in a spreadsheet, picking a winner in a way nobody fully trusts, then chasing down a shipping address. Physical or digital, one-of-one or a bundle — if you can put a price on a ticket, you can raffle it. Pick a template, drop in a photo, and your prize page builds itself.

How it works

1

Pick your prize

Choose a template or start blank, upload a photo, and our AI drafts a clean prize page in seconds.

2

Set price & tickets

Decide what a ticket costs and how many exist. Cap how many one person can buy to keep it fair.

3

Set the deadline

Pick when the raffle closes. Sales stop automatically — you don't have to babysit it.

4

Share one link

Drop your raffle link in chats, stories, and posts. Recruit affiliates to push it for a cut.

5

Draw & deliver

At the deadline we pick a winner fairly, you get paid, and shipping with tracking is built in.

You bring the prize and the audience. Whop brings the tickets, the timer, the fair draw, and the payout.

A draw people can actually trust

The moment that makes or breaks a raffle is the draw. Whop picks the winner using verifiable randomness — every drawing comes with a published proof anyone can check. No "trust me," no off-camera spin. Your buyers can see the odds before they enter and the receipt after you draw.

Built-in, end to end: live odds and entry counts, automatic close at your deadline, a provably-fair winner, instant payout onboarding, and prize shipping with tracking — all without leaving Whop.

Get paid the moment it ends

Ticket revenue lands in your Whop balance as sales happen. Connect your bank once, and payouts run on the same rails creators already use across the platform. When you draw a winner for a physical prize, you collect their address and ship with tracking right from the dashboard.

One important note: raffles and sweepstakes are legally regulated, and the rules vary by region. During beta we're rolling out carefully and reviewing eligibility — check that raffles are available and permitted where you and your audience are before you launch.
